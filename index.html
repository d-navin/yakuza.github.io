ABOUT US

At Yakuza, our goal is to produce the best riding automobiles for the personal commute. We believe that there are lots of untapped engineering talent in the country, they are starving for a greener opportunity. While we invent, manufacture, and sell the vehicles it will create lots of opportunities at all levels for the people who are truly passionate. We want these engineers to follow their hearts to create something useful for the larger community. Today, people unconsciously breathe the exhaust air from fuel-burning motor vehicles. Unfortunately, there is no choice for the people for cleaner air while commuting. As science and scientist around the world confirm that this harmful gas causes serious health hazards to our friends, spouse, mother, father, sisters, brothers, and pets. The damage is limitless. We set out to provide a cleaner choice for commuter vehicles. Yes, we understand that this journey is not a short nor easy one. But it is achievable by today’s youths and leaders of the country. We look forward to making the journey together. It is our way of giving back to create a healthier community.

OUR SERVICES

SUPER-CHARGED MOBILE SERVICING

Being an exclusively electric dealer within India we have found ourselves receiving enquiries through the entirety of the country and currently have many of our customers based as far north as Kashmir and as far south as Kanyakumari. Very quickly we decided it was important to offer a comprehensive, reliable and reasonable nationwide delivery and mobile service to enable the sale of any of our brands within our range to anywhere within the country.

DELIVERY AND DEMOS

New technology is not truly effective if the systems that back it are outdated. Along with our travelling demo days and pop up showrooms we are offering a comprehensive mobile servicing system for electric motorbikes, meaning less cost and downtime for you whilst the minimal service needs your electric motorcycle has are met. Service costings and frequency vary for each brand and model but a general service undertaken at our workshop can be as little as ₹5,000 ex GST.
